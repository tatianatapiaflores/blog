Fried Rice

This recipe has taken us forever to perfect! One of the hardest recipes for us to learn and I'm so glad we can share this easier fried rice with you.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of day old rice
  • 2 cups soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon oyster sauce
  • 3 teaspoons of butter
  • 1 Tablespoon of minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon of chinese 5 spice
  • half a yellow onion
  • 1 12oz packet of frozen vegetables

First cook 4 cups of rice a day before and leave it in the fridge overnight.

Heat a medium size sauce pan with oil in pan. Cut onions, garlic and veggies throw into the pan once oil is hot.

Break up the rice with hands, so there are no clumps. Pro tip wet your hands so the rice doesn't stick. Once broken up throw into the pan with veggies.

Cook rice for 5 mins with the veggies, then add in all the seasonings and sauces. Proceed to cook until rice is fully coated and hot, About 5-10 mins. You can add any meat if you would like or no meat at all. Serve and enjoy!
