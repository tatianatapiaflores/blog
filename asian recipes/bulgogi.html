Bulgogi

Whenever we crave a meal it is this one! If you want a mouth watering meal it is this one!

ingredients

  • 2lbs Thinly sliced beef ribeye
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 cups brown sugar
  • 2 cups soy sauce
  • 1 inch minced ginger
  • 5 cloves mined garlic
  • half of 1 yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup mirin
  • 1 cup of apple sauce or half an asian pear
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

This recipe is very easy and tastes amazing! You can serve with white rice or yakisoba! yakisoba is our favorite with Bulgogi!

First grab a medium size bowl, add equal parts of the water, soy sauce, and sugar. If you increase the amount of meat just make sure those three are equal parts.

Now add in the ginger, garlic, onion, mirin, apple sauce and sesame oil. Add the beef to the bowl, mix around until everything is coated evenly. Cover and put in the fridge for 4 hours or overnight.

Start your sides first because the meat cooks super fast. Grab a medium size saute pan, heating to medium temperature. Throw everything in the bowl in the pan once it is heated. Cook until it is done.

Once done serve with white rice or yakisoba and enjoy!
