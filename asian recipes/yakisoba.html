Yakisoba

This recipe has taken us forever to perfect! One of the hardest recipes for us to learn and I'm so glad we can share this with you.

Ingredients

  • 1lb of yakisoba noodles
  • 2 cups soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon oyster sauce
  • 1 Tablespoon of minced garlic
  • 1/2 a lime
  • half a yellow onion
  • 1 12oz packet of frozen vegetables

Heat a medium size sauce pan with oil in pan. Cut onions, garlic and veggies throw into the pan once oil is hot.

Break up the noodles with hands, so there are no clumps. Once broken up throw into the pan with veggies.

Cook noodles for 5 mins with the veggies, then add in all the seasonings and sauces. Proceed to cook until rice is fully coated and hot, About 5-10 mins. You can add any meat if you would like or no meat at all. Serve and enjoy!
