Potato soup

I somehow stumbled upon this recipe when I was in high school. It is the best potato soup I have ever had!

ingredients

  • 2lbs potatoes
  • ranch seasoning packet
  • 1 cup of heavy whipping cream
  • 2 cups of chedder cheese
  • a dash of garlic powder
  • dash of salt and pepper
  • half a stick of butter

Toppings

  • chopped green onions
  • chedder cheese
  • bacon bits

Start off my rinsing the potatoes and chopping them into little squares. The smaller the squares the faster they cook. Put potatoes in medium size pot with water covering potatoes, place on stove at medium high heat.

Once potatoes are done about 30 mins, drain half of the water. Return to stove add all the seasoning, butter, and heavy cream. Start to mash them up, Mash them until you are happy with the consistency, I mash mine until it is mostly smooth but has some chucks in it.

Serve in a bowl and topping with any of the toppings you like. Its best with all they toppings!
