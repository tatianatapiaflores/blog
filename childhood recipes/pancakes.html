Strawberry blueberry Pancakes

This recipe is one I used to make with my grandma but I have changed it up a bit by added the blueberry muffin mix. This one isn't from sratch, it is a fast way to make the best pancakes.

ingredients

  • 2 cups Preferred pancake mix
  • 1 packet of blueberry muffin mix
  • 1/4 cup strawberry syrup

Lets start off by putting 2 cups of box pancake mix. Follow the instructions on the package. Now it's the fun part add in a whole packet of blueberry muffin mix and the strawberry syrup.

Once mixed all together, use a 1/2 cup to scoop and pour batter on a heated pan. Then top with syrup and enjoy!
