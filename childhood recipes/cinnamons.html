Cinnamon rolls

Cinnamon rolls are my favorite! When I was a kid I would make these with my grandma every weekend.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • granulated sugar
  • salt
  • instant yeast
  • milk
  • water
  • unsalted butter
  • egg

Filling

  • butter
  • brown sugar
  • Cinnamon

Frosting

  • water
  • powdered sugar

Don't be afraid to add anything you want to this recipe! Oreos, nutella, or strawberries you name it you can add it.

Let's start off with the yeast. Heat up the milk until lukewarm. I heated mine up to 102.9.

Pour the yeast into the milk and let it sit for 5 mins or until yeast bubbles up. Then pour the milk yeast in the mixing bowl

Next pour in the ginger ale very slowly. It will fizz up but it goes back down.
