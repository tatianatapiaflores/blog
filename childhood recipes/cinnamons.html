Cinnamon rolls

Cinnamon rolls are my favorite! When I was a kid I would make these with my grandma every weekend.

Ingredients

  • 4 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 2 large egg

Filling

  • 1/2 cup soft unsalted butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons Cinnamon

Frosting

  • 6 ounces cream cheese
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 tablespoon vanilla extract

Don't be afraid to add anything you want to this recipe! Oreos, nutella, or strawberries you name it you can add it.

Preheat oven to 350. Let's start off with the yeast. Heat up the milk until lukewarm. Put yeast and milk into a stand mixer or bowl.

Now add eggs, butter, salt and sugar to the bowl. Mix all together, add dough hook knead for 8 mins.

Roll out dough about 1/2 an inch thick. Spread butter all over dough and add cinnamon/sugar mix. Then roll dough as tight as you can. Once it is rolled cut dough every 1 inch.

Butter a 9x13 inch pan, place cinnamon rolls in pan and let rest of 20 mins. Place in oven and bake for 25-30 mins, serve and enjoy!
