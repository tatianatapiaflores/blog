Enchiladas

This recipe is one of my all time favorites for potlucks!

Ingredients

  • 20 Corn tortillas
  • Salsa verde or any salsa you like
  • Shredded chicken

Put salsa verde in a pan and heat if not made fresh. In a medium saute pan lightly fry the tortillas, then dunk the tortillas in the salsa and place on a plate.

Add the chicken on top of the tortillas and any other toppings. You can add creama, cheese and anything you prefer. Serve and enjoy!
