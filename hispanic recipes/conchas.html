birthday punch

This is a recipe I have had at every birthday party and baby shower since I was a kid. It is the best punch ever!

ingredients

  • 48oz Rainbow sherbet
  • 2 liter of ginger ale
  • Punch bowl and spoon

This punch has 2 simple ingredients. Any flavor of sherbet and any flavor clear soda. How I like my punch is with ginger ale and rainbow sherbet.

First grab a large punch bowl and spoon

Dump in the rainbow sherbet into the punch bowl

Next pour in the ginger ale very slowly. It will fizz up but it goes back down.

That is it! if you want to add more you can add fruit.
