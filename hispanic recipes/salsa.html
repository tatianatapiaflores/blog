Salsa verde

This salsa is great for all of the mexican recipes!

Ingredients

  • 3 tomatillos
  • 4 jalapenos
  • 1-2 cloves of garlic
  • salt to taste

In a medium size sauce pan fill with water and bring to a boil. Once boiling drop in tomatoes and jalapenos.

Once the tomato skin is pulling away from the tomato put the tomatoes in the blender. Take out the jalapenos out of the pan and pull off the stems, put them in the blender too. Add the garlic whole and blender until smooth. Add the water from the pan if salsa is to thick, then add the salt. Blend then taste again.

Serve and enjoy with chips or any dishes you like!
