Tacos

As most of my recipes this one comes from my husband. This is one of my favorite mexican recipes he makes.

Ingredients

  • 2lbs of shredded chicken
  • 20 Corn tortillas

In a hurry we get a rotissary chicken and just shred it. Or you can just boil any chicken you have and shred it.

Then grab the corn tortillas and put all of them in the microwave for 1 min. That way they are easier to roll up.

Heat a medium size saute pan with the bottom covered lightly with oil. Put about a tablespoon of the shredded chicken on a tortilla, roll it up, repeat with the rest of the tortillas. Once oil is hot place rolled tortillas in pan, turn over once one side it toasted.

Serve and enjoy!
